Frequently Asked Questions

What Type of Digital Services Do You Provide?

At Rismic Technology, we offer web design, development, eCommerce solutions, SEO, digital marketing, UI/UX design, and website maintenance. Our services are designed to enhance your online presence and help your business grow.

What Is Your Process for Working with Clients?

Our process starts with an initial consultation to understand your business goals. We then proceed with planning, design, development, and testing, ensuring every aspect is optimized. After launch, we provide ongoing support and marketing strategies to maximize performance.

What Is Your Timeline for Completing a Project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity:
• Basic Websites: 4-6 weeks
• E-commerce Websites: 8-12 weeks
• Custom Web Applications: 12-16+ weeks
We set milestones to ensure smooth progress and timely delivery.

What If I'm Not Satisfied with the Work?

We prioritize client satisfaction through transparent communication, iterative improvements, and milestone approvals. If needed, we offer revisions and additional support to ensure your project meets expectations.

Do You Offer Website Maintenance & Support?

Yes! We provide regular updates, security monitoring, speed optimization, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly and securely.

How Can I Get a Quote for My Project?

Simply contact us via our website, email, or phone. Our team will analyze your requirements and provide a customized quote based on your project's scope and goals.
